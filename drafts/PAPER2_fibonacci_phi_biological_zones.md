In TFA terms:
- F(n-1) = current R-component (relational, immediate)

The ratio F(n)/F(n-1) → φ as n → ∞.

The coupling at Fibonacci saturation:
$$\kappa_{Fib} = \frac{F(n-1)}{F(n-1) + F(n-2)} = \frac{F(n-1)}{F(n)} \to \frac{1}{\phi} = 0.618$$
